# AI Sales Intelligence Agent Team

Generate prospect intelligence and personalized outreach for sales.

## Overview
Researches prospects and crafts personalized outreach.

## Instructions
- Research target company: funding, tech stack, news triggers
- Build ICP-qualified prospect list with contacts
- Generate personalized cold outreach per prospect
- Score leads by fit and intent signals
- Analyze pipeline conversion rates
- Recommend next-best-action per deal
