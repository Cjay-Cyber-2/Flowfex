# AI VC Due Diligence Agent Team

Multi-agent team conducting startup due diligence across market, team, and financials.

## Overview
Coordinated analysis of startup investment opportunities across multiple dimensions.

## Agents
- Market Agent: TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive dynamics
- Team Agent: Founder background, relevant experience
- Financial Agent: Revenue, burn rate, unit economics
- Risk Agent: Regulatory, technical, and market risks

## Instructions
- Accept pitch deck or company profile as input
- Run all agents in parallel across their domain
- Aggregate into unified investment memo
- Output pass/fail recommendation with evidence
