# Chat with Research Papers

Chat with academic papers from ArXiv and other sources.

## Overview
Conversational Q&A over academic research papers.

## Instructions
- Accept paper URLs or search queries (ArXiv, Semantic Scholar)
- Download and parse full paper text
- Chunk by section: abstract, methods, results, discussion
- Embed and index paper content
- Answer questions about methodology, findings, or claims
- Cite specific sections and page numbers
