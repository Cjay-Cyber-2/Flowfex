# Chat with Substack

Search and converse with Substack newsletter archives.

## Overview
Enables Q&A conversations over Substack newsletter archives.

## Instructions
- Accept Substack newsletter URL or author handle
- Fetch and parse published posts via RSS or scrape
- Chunk and embed newsletter content
- Accept questions about topics covered in the newsletter
- Retrieve relevant posts and generate direct answers
- Cite newsletter post titles and publication dates
