# AI Competitor Intelligence Agent Team

Research competitors from multiple angles and synthesize intelligence.

## Overview
Coordinated competitor research across scout, analyst, and synthesizer agents.

## Agents
- Scout: Maps competitive landscape and key players
- Analyst: Deep-dives pricing, features, sentiment
- Synthesizer: Produces competitive intelligence brief

## Instructions
- Accept target company or market segment
- Run all agents sequentially in pipeline
- Output: feature matrix, pricing tiers, SWOT, recommendations
