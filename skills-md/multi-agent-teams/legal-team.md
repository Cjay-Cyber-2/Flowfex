# AI Legal Agent Team

Legal document review, contract analysis, and compliance checking.

## Overview
Specialized legal agents for document review and compliance validation.

## Agents
- Contract Reviewer: Identifies risky clauses and red flags
- Compliance Checker: Validates against GDPR, SOC2
- Summarizer: Plain-English summary for stakeholders

## Instructions
- Accept legal documents or contracts
- Flag high-risk clauses with severity and action
- Output clause review, compliance gaps, executive summary
