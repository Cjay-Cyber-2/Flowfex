# AI Blog Search RAG

Build a semantic search and Q&A system over blog content.

## Overview
Semantic search and question-answering over a blog or article corpus.

## Instructions
- Crawl and parse blog articles into clean text
- Chunk articles by section and embed
- Accept natural language search queries
- Retrieve and rank most relevant articles
- Generate direct answers with article source links
